How to Use

Getting Started

  • Tap Open to load a construction plan
  • Use two fingers to pan and pinch-zoom on the plan

Setting the Scale

  • Tap Set Scale in the toolbar
  • Enter the plan scale directly (e.g. 1" = 20 ft) — or —
  • Tap "calibrate" to tap two points on a known dimension, then enter the real distance
  • Supports Imperial (feet, inches, yards) and Metric (meters, cm, mm)

Units

  • Use the unit dropdown in the top bar to switch display format
  • ft'-in" — feet and inches (e.g., 10'-6")
  • Decimal ft — decimal feet (e.g., 10.50 ft)
  • Metric (m) — meters (e.g., 3.20 m)
  • Changing units recalculates all existing measurements instantly

Measuring

  • Measure — tap two points for a straight-line distance
  • Polyline — tap multiple points for a running total; double-tap or tap "Finish" to complete
  • Drag any measurement point to adjust it

Count

  • Tap Count to enter counting mode
  • Each tap places a numbered marker on the plan
  • Running total is displayed in the status bar and count panel

Area

  • Tap Area to enter area measurement mode (requires scale)
  • Tap 3+ points to define a polygon; double-tap or tap "Finish" to close
  • Shows area in square feet and square yards
  • Optionally enter a depth to calculate cubic yards

Utilities

  • Tap Utility and select a type (Water, Sewer, Storm, Electric)
  • Tap points along the utility run; double-tap or tap "Finish" to complete
  • Adjust Line Width and Opacity in the utility popup
  • Drag any vertex to adjust its position

Editing Points (iPad / iPhone)

  • Long-press (hold ~1 sec) on a utility line to add a point
  • Long-press on a vertex to remove it (if 3+ points)
  • On desktop: right-click instead of long-press

Navigation (iPad / iPhone)

  • Two-finger drag to pan around the plan
  • Pinch to zoom in and out
  • Use < > buttons for multi-page PDFs
  • Press Esc to cancel the current action

Other Tools

  • Compare — overlay two PDF pages to spot differences
  • Export — save your measurements as an image
  • Undo — remove the last measurement or utility line
  • Snaps — toggle snap points extracted from PDF line geometry (endpoints and intersections)

Add to Home Screen

For the best experience on iPad or iPhone, tap the Share button in Safari and select "Add to Home Screen". The app will run full-screen like a native app.
